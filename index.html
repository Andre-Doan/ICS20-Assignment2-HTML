SpaceX

In 2001, Musk conceived Mars Oasis, a project to land a miniature experimental greenhouse on Mars, containing food crops growing on Martian regolith, in an attempt to reawaken public interest in space exploration.
in Octobeer 2001, Musk travelled to Moscow with Jim Cantrell (an aerospace supplies fixer) and Adeo Ressi to buy refurbished Dnepr Intercontinental ballistic missiles to send envisioned payloads into space.Ultimately Musk founded SpaceX in 2002 with a long-term goal of creating a spacefaring civilization. SpaceX develops and manufactures
space launch vehicles with a focus on advancing the state of rocket technology.
The company's first two launch vehicles were the Falcon 1 and Falcon 9 rockets (a nod to Star Wars' Millennium Falcon), and its first spacecraft is the Dragon. In , september 2008, SpaceX's Falcon 1 become the first privately funded liquid-fuelled vehicle to put a sattelite into orbit.
Then In 2006, SpaceX was awarded a contract from NASA to cotinue the development and test of SpaceX Falcon Launch vehicle and dragon Spacecraft in order to transport cargo to the ISS.followed by a US$1.6 billion NASA Commercial Resupply Services program contract on December 23, 2008, for 12 flights of its Falcon 9 rocket and Dragon spacecraft to the Space Station, replacing the US Space Shuttle after it retired in 2011.

Tesla

Tesla Inc(originally Tesla Motors)was incorporated in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning who both palyed a role in the company's early development prior to Elon Musk's involvement.Tesla Motors first built an electric sports car known as The Tesla Roadster, in 2008 with sales of 2500 cars in 31 countries.However, Tesla began delivery of its four-door Model S sedan on June 22, 2012.It unveiled its 3rd product the Model X, aimed at the SUV/minivan market, on February 9, 2012; however, the Model X launch was delayed until September 2015.
